Semester Report

This is a grad school project. I mapped my activities on my laptop from 5 th Sept to 5 th Dec, my first semester at BU. The data mapped consists of the average login time on my laptop. Time spent on each activity such as, Facebook, Spotify or Adobe etc was caollected by minutes, analyzed and categorized into different groups.

Check my data viz in d3

About the project

Macro reads vs Micro Reads

The data shows that my most productive days are Tuesday and Wednesday – when classes starts at 8 am. The result also implies that I started with a strong hold on my subjects but as the semester progressed, I eased up a bit – especially in the second week of every month. That’s also because most critiques get over within the last week of a month.According to the data I have been extremely productive during the last week of November and and the first week of December owing to the finals. This exercise was a proof to myself – how I am more productive when the choice of time is removed. The added pressure and the lack of option of doing it later, made me manage the work load in an efficient manner.
